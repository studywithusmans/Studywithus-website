## Introduction

Exam ka naam sunte hi dil ki dhadkan badh jaati hai? Raat ko neend nahi aati aur padhai me mann nahi lagta? Aap akele nahi hain. Exam stress ek common samasya hai jo lagbhag har student face karta hai. Yeh stress thoda bahut toh theek hai, kyunki yeh aapko padhne ke liye motivate karta hai. Lekin jab **exam stress** had se zyada badh jaata hai, toh yeh aapki performance aur mental health dono ko kharab kar sakta hai. Is article me hum aapko batayenge ki **exam ke tanav ko kaise dur karein**. Hum aapko 10 practical aur aasan tips denge jinhe follow karke aap na sirf stress-free rahenge, balki exam me behtar score bhi kar payenge. Promise karte hain, yeh tips aapki student life ko kaafi aasan bana dengi.
